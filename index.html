About

Two decades behind the lens.

I am a Sydney based photographer with over twenty years chasing light. My work sits between honest portraiture and the unscripted moments of an event, the kind of frames people come back to years later.

I shoot both film and digital, and I like keeping the process relaxed so the pictures feel like you, not a pose. Based in Sydney, happy to travel for the right project.
